# Anglo Windows Master Calculator - Reception User Guide

For: Reception Staff and Front Desk  
Purpose: Quick reference guide for generating customer quotes using the Anglo Windows Master Calculator  
Version: 1.1  
Last Updated: April 19, 2026

## What This Tool Does

The Anglo Windows Master Calculator is a fast, simple way to generate accurate customer quotes. You can:

- Upload architectural drawings and automatically extract product specifications
- Manually enter product details when no drawing is available
- Review and edit extracted information before generating a quote
- Generate a professional mini-quote to send to the customer
- Save quotes for later reference or updates

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open the Anglo Windows Master Calculator in your browser.
2. Click `Sign in with Google`.
3. Select your company Google account.
4. Complete any prompts if asked.
5. You are ready to start generating quotes.

## Two Ways to Start a Quote

### Option 1: Upload a Drawing

Best for: Architectural drawings, building plans, or technical drawings with window and door schedules.

Steps:

1. Click `Upload Drawing` on the home screen.
2. Select a PDF file from your computer.
3. Let the system extract the window and door information.
4. Review the extracted data in the table below.
5. Edit any incorrect information such as dimensions or product type.
6. Click `Confirm & Add to Quote`.
7. The products are added to your quote workspace.

What gets extracted:

- Product code such as `W1` or `D1`
- Width and height dimensions
- Product type such as window or door
- Finish and glass type if visible
- Safety glass requirements

What you may need to correct:

- Dimensions if the drawing is unclear
- Product type if the system cannot auto-detect it
- Quantity if there are multiple identical units

### Option 2: Manual Entry

Best for: Quick quotes, phone orders, or when you do not have a drawing.

Steps:

1. Click `Manual Entry` on the home screen.
2. Fill in the quote header:

- Quote Number: Auto-generated or entered manually
- Customer Name
- Date: Auto-filled with today's date
- Phone Number: Optional
- Address: Optional
- Salesperson: Auto-filled with your name
- Installer: Optional
- Notes: Any special instructions

3. Click `Next` to start adding products.

## Adding Products to Your Quote

### Step 1: Select Product Type

1. Click `Add Product` or `Add Another`.
2. Choose the product group:

- Casement: Fixed and hinged windows
- Sliding Window: Elite or Knysna sliders
- Patio Door: Sliding patio doors
- Palace Door: Heavy-duty sliding doors
- Shopfront: Commercial shopfronts and hinged doors
- Folding Door: Vistafold folding or stacking doors
- Frameless: Frameless folding or balustrade systems

3. Choose the specific system within that group.

### Step 2: Enter Dimensions

For most products:

- Width (mm)
- Height (mm)
- Quantity

For folding doors:

- Width per leaf (mm)
- Height (mm)
- Number of leaves
- Quantity

For frameless balustrades:

- Run length (metres)
- Quantity

### Step 3: Select Configuration

Choose the options for the product:

- Hinge type for casements: Top-hung, side-hung, or fixed
- Panel count for sliders
- Door type for shopfronts
- Glass type
- Safety glass
- Frame colour
- Hardware colour

### Step 4: Review and Confirm

The system automatically calculates the price based on:

- Dimensions
- Product type and configuration
- Glass type and colour
- Hardware options

You will see:

- Unit Price
- Quantity
- Line Total

Click `Add to Quote` to add the product.

## Managing Your Quote

### Viewing Your Quote Summary

The quote summary panel shows:

- Quote Number
- Customer Name
- Line Items
- Subtotal
- Markup
- Total

### Editing Products

1. Find the product in the quote summary.
2. Click `Edit`.
3. Change the details.
4. Click `Update`.
5. The total recalculates automatically.

### Removing Products

1. Find the product in the quote summary.
2. Click the delete button.
3. Confirm deletion.
4. The total recalculates automatically.

### Adjusting Markup

1. Find the `Markup` field in the quote summary.
2. Enter the new percentage.
3. The total recalculates automatically.

### Applying a Discount

1. Find the `Discount` field in the quote summary.
2. Enter the discount amount or percentage.
3. The total recalculates automatically.

## Generating and Exporting Quotes

### Preview Your Quote

1. Click `Preview Quote`.
2. Check the customer information, products, pricing, and total.

### Export as PDF

1. Click `Export as PDF`.
2. The quote is generated with Anglo Windows branding.
3. Your browser downloads the PDF.
4. You can print it or email it to the customer.

### Email Quote to Customer

1. Click `Email Quote`.
2. Enter the customer email address.
3. Add a message if needed.
4. Click `Send`.

### Save Quote for Later

1. Click `Save Quote`.
2. The quote is saved in the system.
3. Retrieve it later by quote number or customer name.
4. Edit and resend it whenever needed.

## Reviewing Extracted Data From PDF Upload

When you upload a drawing, the system extracts product information into a review table.

### Table Columns

- Code: Product identifier from the drawing
- Product Type: What the system detected
- Width (mm): Extracted horizontal dimension
- Height (mm): Extracted vertical dimension
- Finish: Material finish
- Glass: Detected glass type
- Qty: Quantity
- Status: Valid or needs review

### Editing Extracted Data

1. Click any cell in the table to edit it.
2. Use dropdowns for fields like Product Type, Glass, and Finish.
3. Type numeric corrections directly into Width, Height, or Qty.
4. Red highlighting means the value is missing or invalid.
5. Fix all highlighted fields before confirming.

### Confirming Extracted Data

1. Review all rows in the table.
2. Correct any errors or missing information.
3. Click `Confirm & Add to Quote`.
4. The extracted products are added to your quote workspace.

## Tips and Tricks

### Speed Up Data Entry

- Use `Tab` to move between fields
- Copy and paste dimensions where sizes repeat
- Use keyboard shortcuts:
- `Ctrl+S` to save
- `Ctrl+P` to print
- `Ctrl+E` to export as PDF

### Avoid Common Mistakes

- Always check that dimensions are in millimetres
- Confirm the product type matches the customer request
- Double-check the glass type
- Do not forget safety glass when required
- Do not leave quantity as zero

### Quality Checks Before Sending

Verify:

- Customer name is correct
- Dimensions are realistic
- Product types match the request or drawing
- Glass type is correct
- Quantity is correct
- Total price looks reasonable
- Quote number is unique

## Troubleshooting

### Dimensions Are Missing or Invalid

Problem: The system will not let you confirm a product.

Solution:

- Check that width and height are greater than zero
- Make sure dimensions are in millimetres
- If extracted from a drawing, verify them manually

### Product Type Not Recognized

Problem: The system could not auto-detect the product type.

Solution:

- Select the product type manually
- Ask your supervisor if you are unsure

### Quote Will Not Save

Problem: The save button does not work.

Solution:

- Make sure all required fields are completed
- Check your internet connection
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact IT support if the issue continues

### PDF Export Is Blank or Incomplete

Problem: The exported PDF is missing information.

Solution:

- Confirm all products are included in the quote summary
- Try the export again
- Take a screenshot and send it to your supervisor if the issue continues

## FAQ

**Can I generate a quote without a drawing?**  
Yes. Use `Manual Entry` to enter customer details and products manually.

**What if the drawing has products I do not recognize?**  
Check the drawing legend or ask your supervisor. You can also choose the closest product type manually.

**Can I edit a quote after saving it?**  
Yes. Use `Load Quote`, make changes, and save again.

**How long does a quote stay in the system?**  
Indefinitely. Quotes can be retrieved by quote number or customer name.

**Can I generate a quote for a customer I have quoted before?**  
Yes. Load the earlier quote and use `Save as New` to create a fresh version.

**What if I send a quote with a mistake?**  
Load the quote, correct it, and send an updated version. Add a note such as `Updated Quote`.

**Can multiple people work on the same quote?**  
Not at the same time. One person should save first, then the next person can load and continue.
