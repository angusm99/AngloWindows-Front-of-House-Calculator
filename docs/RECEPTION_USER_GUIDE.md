# Front of House Calculator-1 - Reception User Guide

**For:** Reception Staff & Front Desk  
**Purpose:** Quick reference guide for generating quotes using Front of House Calculator-1  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## What This Tool Does

**Front of House Calculator-1** is a fast, simple way to generate accurate quotes for customers. You can:

- **Upload architectural drawings** and automatically extract product specifications
- **Manually enter product details** if you don't have a drawing
- **Review and edit extracted information** before generating a quote
- **Generate a professional mini-quote** to send to the customer
- **Save quotes** for later reference or updates

---

## Getting Started

### Logging In

1. Open Front of House Calculator-1 in your browser
2. Click **"Sign in with Google"** (or your company SSO)
3. Enter your credentials
4. You're ready to start generating quotes

## Two Ways to Start a Quote

### Option 1: Upload a Drawing (Fastest)

**Best for:** Architectural drawings, building plans, or technical drawings with window/door schedules

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Upload Drawing"** on the home screen
2. Select a PDF file from your computer (architectural plans, window schedules, etc.)
3. The system automatically extracts window and door information
4. Review the extracted data in the table below
5. Edit any incorrect information (dimensions, product type, etc.)
6. Click **"Confirm & Add to Quote"**
7. The products are added to your quote workspace

**What Gets Extracted:**
- Product code (W1, D1, etc.)
- Width and height dimensions
- Product type (window, door, etc.)
- Finish and glass type (if visible)
- Safety glass requirements

**What You May Need to Correct:**
- Dimensions (if the drawing is unclear)
- Product type (if the system can't auto-detect)
- Quantity (if multiple identical units)

---

### Option 2: Manual Entry

**Best for:** Quick quotes, phone orders, or when you don't have a drawing

**Steps:**

1. Click **"Manual Entry"** on the home screen
2. Fill in the quote header:
   - **Quote Number** (auto-generated or enter manually)
   - **Customer Name**
   - **Date** (auto-filled with today's date)
   - **Phone Number** (optional)
   - **Address** (optional)
   - **Salesperson** (auto-filled with your name)
   - **Installer** (optional)
   - **Notes** (any special instructions)
3. Click **"Next"** to start adding products

## Adding Products to Your Quote

### Step 1: Select Product Type

1. Click **"Add Product"** or **"Add Another"**
2. Choose the **product group**:
   - **Casement** – Fixed and hinged windows (30.5mm, 38mm profiles)
   - **Sliding Window** – Elite or Knysna sliders
   - **Patio Door** – Sliding patio doors (domestic)
   - **Palace Door** – Heavy-duty sliding doors
   - **Shopfront** – Commercial shopfronts and hinged doors
   - **Folding Door** – Vistafold folding/stacking doors
   - **Frameless** – Frameless folding or balustrade systems

3. Choose the **specific system** within that group (e.g., "Elite" for Sliding Window)

### Step 2: Enter Dimensions

**For most products (Casement, Sliders, Shopfront, etc.):**
- **Width (mm)** – Horizontal dimension
- **Height (mm)** – Vertical dimension
- **Quantity** – How many units of this size

**For Folding Doors (Vistafold):**
- **Width per leaf (mm)** – Width of each folding panel
- **Height (mm)** – Vertical dimension
- **Number of Leaves** – How many panels (3–8)
- **Quantity** – How many folding door units

**For Frameless Balustrade (Crystal View):**
- **Run Length (metres)** – Total length of the balustrade
- **Quantity** – How many balustrade runs

### Step 3: Select Configuration

Choose the options for this product:

- **Hinge Type** (for casements): Top-hung, Side-hung, Fixed
- **Panel Count** (for sliders): 2, 3, 4, or 6 panels
- **Door Type** (for shopfronts): Single hinged, Double hinged, Stable door, Pivot
- **Glass Type**: 4mm Clear, Laminated, Double-glazed (DGU), Tinted, Obscured
- **Safety Glass**: Yes/No (required for certain applications)
- **Frame Colour**: White, Black, Anodised, Custom
- **Hardware Colour**: Matching frame, Contrast colour

### Step 4: Review & Confirm

The system automatically calculates the price for this product based on:
- Dimensions
- Product type and configuration
- Glass type and colour
- Hardware options

You'll see:
- **Unit Price** – Price per unit
- **Quantity** – Number of units
- **Line Total** – Unit price × quantity

Click **"Add to Quote"** to add this product to your quote.

## Managing Your Quote

### Viewing Your Quote Summary

On the right side of the screen, you'll see a **Quote Summary** panel showing:

- **Quote Number** – Unique identifier
- **Customer Name** – Who the quote is for
- **Line Items** – Each product with its price
- **Subtotal** – Sum of all products
- **Markup** – Percentage markup (default 20%)
- **Total** – Final quote price

### Editing Products

1. Find the product in the quote summary
2. Click the **"Edit"** button next to it
3. Change any details (dimensions, quantity, configuration)
4. Click **"Update"** to save changes
5. The total automatically recalculates

### Removing Products

1. Find the product in the quote summary
2. Click the **"Delete"** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm deletion
4. The product is removed and total recalculates

### Adjusting Markup

1. In the Quote Summary, find the **Markup** field
2. Enter a new percentage (e.g., 15%, 25%, 30%)
3. The total automatically recalculates

### Applying a Discount

1. In the Quote Summary, find the **Discount** field
2. Enter a discount amount or percentage
3. The total automatically recalculates

1. Click **"Email Quote"**

3. You can retrieve it later by quote number or customer name

## Reviewing Extracted Data (From PDF Upload)

When you upload a drawing, the system extracts product information into a **review table**. Here's how to use it:

### Understanding the Table

| Column | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Code** | Product identifier from the drawing (e.g., W1, D1) |
| **Product Type** | What the system detected (Window, Door, etc.) |
| **Width (mm)** | Horizontal dimension extracted from the drawing |
| **Height (mm)** | Vertical dimension extracted from the drawing |
| **Finish** | Material finish (e.g., white, anodised, etc.) |
| **Glass** | Glass type detected (e.g., clear, DGU, etc.) |
| **Qty** | Quantity (usually 1 unless marked otherwise) |
| **Status** | ✓ (Valid) or ⚠ (Warning – needs review) |

### Editing Extracted Data

1. Click on any cell in the table to edit it
2. For dropdown fields (Product Type, Glass, Finish), click to select from options
3. For numeric fields (Width, Height, Qty), type the correct value
4. Red highlighting indicates missing or invalid data
5. Fix all red-highlighted cells before confirming

### Confirming Extracted Data

1. Review all rows in the table
2. Fix any errors or missing information
3. Click **"Confirm & Add to Quote"** at the bottom
4. All extracted products are added to your quote workspace

## Common Tasks

### Generate a Quick Quote (5 minutes)

1. Click **"Upload Drawing"**
2. Select a PDF with a window/door schedule
3. Review extracted data (should be mostly correct)
4. Click **"Confirm & Add to Quote"**
5. Review the quote summary
6. Click **"Export as PDF"**
7. Send to customer

### Quote from a Phone Call

1. Click **"Manual Entry"**
2. Enter customer details
3. Add products manually (one at a time)
4. Review total
5. Click **"Export as PDF"**
6. Email or print for customer

### Update an Existing Quote

1. Click **"Load Quote"**
2. Enter the quote number or customer name
3. The quote loads in the workspace
4. Edit any products or details
5. Click **"Save"** to update
6. Click **"Export as PDF"** to send updated version

### Generate Multiple Quotes for One Customer

1. Create the first quote
2. Click **"Save as New"** instead of "Save"
3. Edit details for the second quote
4. Save with a new quote number
5. Both quotes are saved separately

## Tips & Tricks

### Speed Up Data Entry

- **Use Tab key** to move between fields instead of clicking
- **Copy & Paste** dimensions if multiple products are the same size
- **Use keyboard shortcuts:**
  - `Ctrl+S` to save
  - `Ctrl+P` to print
  - `Ctrl+E` to export as PDF

### Avoid Common Mistakes

- ✓ Always check dimensions are in **millimetres** (not metres)
- ✓ Confirm the **product type** matches what the customer ordered
- ✓ Double-check **glass type** (DGU vs. single-pane makes a big difference in price)
- ✗ Don't forget to select **safety glass** if required
- ✗ Don't leave **quantity** as zero

### Quality Checks Before Sending

Before exporting a quote, always verify:
- [ ] Customer name is correct
- [ ] All dimensions are realistic (not 0 or 99999)
- [ ] Product types match the drawing or customer request
- [ ] Glass type is correct
- [ ] Quantity is correct
- [ ] Total price looks reasonable
- [ ] Quote number is unique

## Troubleshooting

### "Dimensions are missing or invalid"

**Problem:** The system won't let you confirm a product.

**Solution:**
- Check that Width and Height are both greater than 0
- Make sure dimensions are in millimetres (mm), not metres
- If extracted from a drawing, manually verify the dimensions are correct

### "Product type not recognized"

**Problem:** The system couldn't auto-detect the product type from the drawing.

**Solution:**
- Manually select the product type from the dropdown
- If unsure, ask your supervisor or check the drawing legend

### "Quote won't save"

**Problem:** The save button doesn't work.

**Solution:**
- Make sure all required fields are filled (customer name, at least one product)
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page and trying again
- Contact IT support if the problem persists

### "PDF export is blank or incomplete"

**Problem:** The exported PDF doesn't show all the information.

**Solution:**
- Make sure all products are added to the quote (check the summary panel)
- Try exporting again
- If the problem persists, take a screenshot and send it to your supervisor

## FAQ

**Q: Can I generate a quote without a drawing?**  
A: Yes! Use the "Manual Entry" option to enter customer details and products manually.

**Q: What if the drawing has products I don't recognize?**  
A: Check the drawing legend or ask your supervisor. You can also manually select the closest product type.

**Q: Can I edit a quote after saving it?**  
A: Yes! Click "Load Quote" to find it, make changes, and save again.

**Q: How long does a quote stay in the system?**  
A: Indefinitely. All quotes are saved and can be retrieved by quote number or customer name.

**Q: Can I generate a quote for a customer I've quoted before?**  
A: Yes! Load their previous quote and use "Save as New" to create a new version.

**Q: What if I make a mistake and send a quote?**  
A: Load the quote, make corrections, and send an updated version. Include a note saying "Updated Quote" in the email.

**Q: Can multiple people work on the same quote?**  
A: Not simultaneously. Save your work, and the next person can load it and continue.

**Q: How do I know if my quote is competitive?**  
A: That's a sales question for your supervisor. The calculator generates quotes based on your company's standard pricing.
